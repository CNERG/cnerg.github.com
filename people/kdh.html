Graduate Appointment¶

I am an Office of Science Laboratory Graduate appointee at Argonne National Lab. This program allows me to closely collaborate with the DOE Used Fuel Disposition campaign while I produce my dissertation. Within the context of CNERG, I am a research assistant developing simulation tools and methods for analysis of advanced nuclear fuel cycles.
